1.3 pitch > scales and modes

Scales are collections of pitches.
Modes are collections of pitches coupled with their melodic and harmonic characteristics.

1.4 pitch > melodic lines

Melodic lines are collections of pitches with motion primarily through the temporal axis.

1.5 pitch > harmonic sets

Harmonic sets are collections of pitches with motion primarily through the frequential axis.

1.6 pitch > functional harmony

Functional harmony is the description of harmony as functional relations between harmonic sets.

1.7 pitch > expressive harmony

Expressive harmony is the description of harmony as the expressive qualities of harmonic sets.

1.8 pitch > melody and harmony

Pitch is the interplay between melody and harmony.
